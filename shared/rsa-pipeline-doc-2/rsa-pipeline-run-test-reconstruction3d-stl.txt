The Reconstruction3D-stl executable recon-v4-stl locates at the /usr/local/bin/reconstruction3D-stl folder

Unzip rsa-pipeline-reconstruction3d-stl-test-data.tar.gz to some folder [my_folder] on your computer.


option = 5
Voxels = [my_folder]/rsa-pipeline-reconstruction3d-stl-test-data/OsRILp00001d12_rootwork.out
OutputStl= [my_folder]/rsa-pipeline-reconstruction3d-stl-test-data/OsRILp00001d12_rootwork.out.stl
"ExtraInfo" = "Extra Information"

Type in the terminal window:

/usr/local/bin/reconstruction3D-stl/recon-v4-stl  5 [my_folder]/rsa-pipeline-reconstruction3d-stl-test-data/OsRILp00001d12_rootwork.out [my_folder]/rsa-pipeline-reconstruction3d-stl-test-data/OsRILp00001d12_rootwork.out.stl "Extra Information"

On my computer, I chose for test:

[my_folder] = ~/tmp

So, 

/usr/local/bin/reconstruction3D-stl/recon-v4-stl  5 ~/tmp/rsa-pipeline-reconstruction3d-stl-test-data/OsRILp00001d12_rootwork.out ~/tmp/rsa-pipeline-reconstruction3d-stl-test-data/OsRILp00001d12_rootwork.out.stl "Extra Information"

You will see in the terminal window something like:
